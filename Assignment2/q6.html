Vada Pav Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • pav buns
  • medium-sized potatoes mashed
  • besan
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt, turmeric

Steps:

  1. Prepare spiced mashed potato filling.
  2. Make a batter with gram flour and water.
  3. Dip potato filling in batter and deep-fry to make vada.
  4. Serve in pav buns with chutneys.
